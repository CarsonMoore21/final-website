Monkey-ing Around

Humor is basically my default setting. I can turn almost anything into a joke, even the most random stuff. I’ve found that making people laugh is the easiest way to get through the day especially when things get stressful. It’s like a little achievement every time someone cracks up at one of my jokes. Honestly, I’m just here for the laughs. The world can be heavy sometimes, so why not lighten things up with a joke? Plus, it’s way more fun than taking everything so seriously.

Why did the monkey start a podcast?

Because he was really good at monkeying around with ideas.
