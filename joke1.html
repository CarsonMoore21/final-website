Paperback vs Kindle

My partner got me a Kindle for my birthday. I cried when she gave me it full on tears. It was the most thoughtful gift I have ever received. I am a huge reader and a Kindle was something that I had wanted for years, but every time I would come close to buying it I decided not to since I enjoyed books in a physical form. I have a goal to read 50 books this year I am currently at 44 and at least 30 of those were read on my kindle. While I do still enjoy physical books I think I enjoy my kindle much more.


Paperback: "I’ve got a character—every coffee ring tells a story!"

Kindle: "I’ve got a case—just in case you spill something on me."
